# Multiverse System Architecture

## Overview
This document outlines the complete architecture of the multiverse for our game, detailing the universe directory, travel mechanics, and connection rules.

## Universe Directory
Each universe within the multiverse can be categorized based on its theme, environment, and unique characteristics. The universes are as follows:

1. **Universe Alpha**
   - **Theme**: Science Fiction
   - **Environment**: Futuristic cities, advanced technology
   - **Characteristics**: AI beings, interstellar travel
   
2. **Universe Beta**
   - **Theme**: Fantasy
   - **Environment**: Magical realms, mystical creatures
   - **Characteristics**: Magic systems, ancient prophecies
   
3. **Universe Gamma**
   - **Theme**: Horror
   - **Environment**: Dark forests, haunted landscapes
   - **Characteristics**: Supernatural entities, survival challenges

## Travel Mechanics
Travel between universes can be achieved through various means:

- **Portals**: Magical or technological gateways that connect two different universes.
- **Warp Drives**: Spaceships equipped with warp drives that can traverse multiversal space.
- **Rituals**: Specific rituals or spells that allow characters to step into different dimensions.

### Rules for Travelling
1. **Energy Source**: All travel methods require a power source that could be energy crystals, magical artifacts, or technological fuel.
2. **Cool-down Period**: After traveling, there is a cool-down period before a character can travel again.
3. **Identification**: Each character must possess a unique identifier across the multiverse to prevent disorientation during travel.

## Connection Rules
When connecting different universes, certain rules must be enforced to maintain balance and prevent chaos:

1. **No Interference**: Characters from one universe should not interfere with the natural events of another universe.
2. **Knowledge Limitation**: Characters can only retain knowledge specific to their home universe unless they have acquired special abilities.
3. **Unity Council**: A governing body that oversees connections between universes, ensuring peace and coordination.

## Conclusion
The multiverse system aims to create a rich, interconnected gameplay experience where players can explore diverse realities while adhering to the fundamental rules and mechanics of the multiverse.
